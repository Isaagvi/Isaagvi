Monaco's culture

Monaco has a Mediterranean, Roman Catholic culture emphasizing the family; deeply intertwined with its aristocratic heritage and royal traditions. The Grimaldi family has ruled Monaco for over 700 years, and their influence can be seen in the principality's architecture, art, and ceremonies.
